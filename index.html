Unfortunately, it is impossible to be around your family all the time and be able to take the necessary measures. In this respect personal emergency response systems are pretty much the solution to many people.

They are very helpful in such cases when a person has got heart problems, or seizures, or else and there is a possibility of losing conscience and/or falling. The alert devices will do exactly what is needed to be done in such cases – they notify the closet person so that aid could be delivered and fast.

The history of medical alert systems dates back to 70s when their first analogue was introduced. At that time it was a very simple device that looked like a push button and it was mostly supposed to be worn around a person’s neck. The signal from it was transmitted to some base station which in its turn was connected to a house phone like. The system was pretty simple and it meant to send an alert signal to the operator of the call center, the latter then forwarded it to the relevant medical assistance option.

At the present moment the devices have become much more sophisticated and varied and it is possible to place them not only at a person’s neck, but also at their wrist and even all over the house, if necessary. Some modern devices also allow two-side communication and other most sensitive ones are able to detect a fall on their own and send a signal for help on their own accord.

Medical alert systems are mostly aimed to help people with chronic illness, serious health condition that requires constant monitoring and senior people who suffer from various health problems. They are aimed to prevent a person being left unattended in the situation of falling or any situation in general that needs immediate medical or other assistance. The devices can also be helpful in cases of memory loss and problems with eyesight when a person is not in the immediate danger or emergency; however, might need a certain kind of assistance. In this case close relatives or friends will be notified at once.

Choosing a Medical Alert System right for you

The choice of various medical alert systems is very broad nowadays and the devices differ in purpose and complexity of functions. It is important to understand who you purchase the device for, their health condition and requirements, and what is it exactly that you expect from the device.

  • There are special systems that are only work for a certain disability, for instance, the ones for people with the history of stroke or heart attack; or that react for seizures, or else.
  • The devices also differ with regards to the place on a person’s body – they can be pendants or wristbands. Both options can be either suitable or unsuitable in different cases depending on a person’s health condition; thus, this aspect also requires consideration.
  • Some medical alert systems presuppose additional buttons placement around the house, if necessary.
  • There is always a choice of contacts for help, in case one is needed. The list should also be thought through carefully and the more people nearby can be reached, the better.
  • It is important to make sure that each package has got a set of backup batteries and that they are stored somewhere at hand.
  • The modern devices include a portable device and a base station – it is important to make sure that a base station can be reached from every point of your yard and house.
  • One of the crucial parts in choosing a medical alert system is making sure that the company has got their own monitoring center as well as their own call center operators. This simplifies, speeds up contact process as well as speaks for reliability.
  • It should also be paid attention that the center has had a certification of Underwriters Laboratories (UL).
